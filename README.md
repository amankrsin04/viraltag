# 🚀 ViralTag AI

An AI-powered Instagram hashtag research tool that helps creators discover viral, growth-focused, and niche hashtags for their content.

🌐 Live Demo: https://amankrsin04.github.io/viraltag/

---

## ✨ Features

### 🔥 AI Hashtag Analysis

Enter any video topic such as:

* Fitness
* Motivation
* Trading
* AI Tools
* Coding
* Education
* Food
* Travel

and instantly generate optimized hashtags.

### 📈 Smart Hashtag Categories

Hashtags are automatically grouped into:

* Viral Hashtags (High Reach)
* Growth Hashtags (Medium Competition)
* Niche Hashtags (Easy Ranking)

### 🎯 Viral Score

Get a predicted viral potential score based on:

* Topic relevance
* Reach potential
* Growth opportunities

### ✍ AI Caption Generator

Automatically generates a ready-to-post caption for your niche.

### 📊 Reach Estimation

View estimated reach ranges for generated hashtags.

### ⏰ Best Posting Time

Receive recommended posting windows to maximize engagement.

### 🚀 Growth Tips

Actionable content growth recommendations for creators.

### 📋 One-Click Copy

* Copy individual hashtags
* Copy captions
* Copy all hashtags instantly
